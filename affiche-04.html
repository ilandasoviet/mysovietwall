Affiche 04

Titre
Нет!
Artiste
Viktor Ivanovich Govorkov
Date
1954
Texte / traduction
Soviet social poster “No!” has an anti-alcohol trend. The poster was painted by the artist V.I. Govorkov in 1954, with a purpose of propaganda against drunkenness and alcoholism. The poster shows a well-dressed man who is being offered to have a shot, while having a dinner, but with a gesture of his hand he refuses the glass with vodka. The name of the poster speaks for itself – “No!” to alcohol. The poster is made in the style of social realism.
